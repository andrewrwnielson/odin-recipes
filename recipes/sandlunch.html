Turkey Sandwich Meal

Description

This turkey sandwich meal is packed with protein and easy to take on the go! The sweetness of the fruit and greek yogurt constrasts deliciously with the savory taste of the meat sticks and sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Bread
  • Fat-Free Turkey
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Light Greek Yogurt
  • Turkey Sausage Sticks
  • Roma Tomato

Steps

  1. Arrange turkey, cheese, and sliced tomatoes on bread
  2. Apply mustard and close sandwich
