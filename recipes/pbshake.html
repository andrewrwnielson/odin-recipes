Peanut Butter Protein Shake Recipe

Description

This chocolate peanut butter banana protein shake is a quick and easy-to-make healthy drink that is packed with protein. It’s perfect for those who are always on the go and want to stay healthy. It’s a delicious way to get your daily dose of protein and other essential nutrients.

Ingredients

  • PB Fit
  • Frozen Banana
  • Unsweetened Vanilla Almond Milk
  • Chocolate Protein Powder
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to blender
  2. Blend for {} seconds
  3. Pour into container and enjoy!
