Egg White Omelette Meal

Description

This omelette is a healthy and delicious meal option and is low in calories and high in protein.

Ingredients

  • Egg Whites
  • Diced Ham
  • Frozen Spinach
  • Frozen Pepper and Onion Mix
  • Turkey Sausage Patties

Steps

  1. Put sausages on a plate and wrap in paper towel
  2. Put in microwave for 60 seconds
  3. Prepare pan on medium heat and apply cooking spray
  4. Cook ham in pan fully
  5. Set ham aside and apply cooking spray to pan again
  6. Cook vegetables in pan fully
  7. Pour egg whites in the same pan and cook until bubbly
  8. Flip and let cook for 1 minute
  9. Put ham on omelette and fold in half
  10. Let cook for 1 minute
  11. Grab sausage out of microwave and prepare food on plate
