Scrambled Egg Whites and Vegetables Meal

Description

This dish is a healthy and delicious meal option and is low in calories and high in protein.

Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp. - Egg Whites
  • 2.5 oz. - Diced Ham
  • 0.5 cup - Frozen Spinach
  • 0.5 cup - Frozen Pepper and Onion Mix
  • 2 - Turkey Sausage Patties

Steps

  1. Put sausages on a plate and wrap in paper towel
  2. Put in microwave for 45 seconds
  3. Prepare pan on medium heat and apply cooking spray
  4. Cook ham and vegetables together in pan fully
  5. Pour egg whites into pan and scramble
  6. Prepare eggs and sausage on plate
